About Me

Hi! I’m Prince Visanguliryo a passionate and versatile Data Scientist, Analyst, and Web
Designer with a strong technical foundation in data science, web development, artificial
intelligence, industrial electronics, and automotive mechanics.

I’m proficient in a wide range of tools and technologies including Python, SQL, JavaScript,
React, Node.js, Tableau, Power BI, PostgreSQL, Java, HTML, CSS, and C++. I specialize in
turning data into actionable insights to optimize operations, solve real-world problems, and
support business growth. In addition, I have a solid background in graphic design, allowing
me to blend analytical thinking with creative presentation.

My Journey

I was born and raised in Goma, a beautiful town in North Kivu, Democratic Republic of Congo.
I earned my high school diploma in Industrial Electronics from École du Cinquantenaire. After
high school, I moved to Kinshasa, where I spent over a year learning English at CALI (Congo
American Language Institute), one of the top English centers in DRC. During that time, I also
pursued hands-on training in automotive mechanics, driven by my curiosity about how cars work.

My university journey began in 2022 when I moved to Kenya to join United States International
University-Africa (USIU-A), where I’m currently pursuing a Bachelor’s degree in Data Science
and Analytics. Since then, I’ve continued to grow by exploring web development, graphic design,
piano playing (still learning!), and enrolling in various online courses to continuously upskill.

My work and Vision

In February 2024, I launched my own company Phoenix Business to merge my passion for
